Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Bellybuttons may host bacteria that can be used for creating synthetic beef! We have taken samples from volunteers to find out how much of each bacteria is found in their belly buttons.

Below you'll find three charts. Pick the test subject from the first drop down box, and you'll see the demographic info of the selected volunteer. The first chart displays the top ten bacteria found in their bellybuttons, next to the chart of how often the volunteer washed their bellybutton. The third chart is another visualization showing the spread of all the bacterium found from the sample.
